About BNBE Technologies

BNBE Technologies is an electronics development and manufacturing firm focused on LPG conversion systems and access-control electronics. With in-house SMT assembly, firmware, and rigorous QC, we deliver reliable, serviceable hardware for installers, conversion centers, and fleet operators.

  • In-house Manufacturing — PCB assembly, firmware, and enclosures.
  • Fast Support — local engineering, training, and after-sales.
  • Compliance Roadmap — CE/ISO plans aligned with export goals.
